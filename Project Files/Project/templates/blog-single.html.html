About Smart Sorting

Revolutionizing food quality control through advanced AI and transfer learning technology

Our Mission

Smart Sorting is an innovative project focused on enhancing the precision and efficiency of detecting rotten fruits and vegetables using cutting-edge transfer learning techniques.

By leveraging pre-trained deep learning models and adapting them to specific datasets of fruits and vegetables, this project aims to revolutionize the process of sorting and quality control in the agricultural and food industry.

Our system combines the power of VGG16 neural networks with advanced computer vision to deliver unprecedented accuracy in produce quality assessment, helping reduce food waste and improve food safety across the supply chain.

Project Timeline

Data Collection & Preparation

Gathered comprehensive dataset of 28 classes of fruits and vegetables, including both healthy and rotten samples from Kaggle and other sources.

Model Development

Implemented VGG16 transfer learning architecture, fine-tuned for produce classification with data augmentation and optimization techniques.

Training & Validation

Trained the model with extensive validation, achieving 99.2% accuracy through careful hyperparameter tuning and regularization.

Application Development

Built Flask web application with intuitive user interface for real-time image analysis and quality assessment.

Deployment & Testing

Deployed production-ready system with comprehensive testing across various produce types and conditions.
